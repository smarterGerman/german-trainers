-tion / -sion Trainer

Learn the spelling of words ending in -tion or -sion

-tion (Nation)
-sion (Vision)

Both -tion and -sion are pronounced the same way in German: /tsion/. This makes spelling tricky!

-tion is much more common: Nation, Situation, Information, Funktion
-sion usually comes after l, n, r: Version, Explosion, Dimension, Pension

Listen to each word and choose the correct spelling. Remember: the pronunciation sounds identical!
