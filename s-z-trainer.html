s / z Spelling Trainer

Learn when German words start with s or z

s = voiced /z/ sound
z = /ts/ sound

German s at the start of a word (before vowels) sounds like English "z" (voiced). German z sounds like "ts" in "bits". This trainer helps you recognize which spelling is correct.

Examples:
- s: Sonne, sehen, Saft, sagen (voiced /z/)
- z: Zeit, zehn, Zug, zahlen (/ts/)
Think: Does the word start with a /ts/ sound or a /z/ sound?
