s or z?

Listening Trainer
Space Play F s J z
The sounds:

s = voiced /z/ sound (like English "z" in "zoo")
When s starts a syllable before a vowel: Son|ne, se|hen, le|sen, Na|se
At word end: voiceless /s/ (like English "s"): Haus, Bus, Glas
Before consonants: voiceless /s/: Skulptur, Maske

z = always /ts/ sound (like "ts" in "bits")
Never changes - start, middle, or end: Zeit, Rezept, Herz

Tip: If you hear /ts/, it's z. If you hear a buzzing /z/ (English "z"), it's German s.

Note: sp/st at syllable start sound like "shp/sht" - practiced in a separate trainer.
