F vs V - How to distinguish:

Both F and V are pronounced /f/ in German. You can't hear the difference - you have to memorize which words use V.

V - only in these cases:
  • prefixes: ver-, vor-, voll-
  • ending -iv: aktiv, naiv, positiv
  • 12 words: Vater, Vetter, Vogel, Vieh, Veilchen, Volk, Nerv, viel, vier, voll, von, vor
    Mnemonic: "Vater, Vetter, Vogel, Vieh, Veilchen, Volk, Nerv vergess ich nie"
    viel|leicht, viel|malsare related toviel
    vier|zehn, Vier|telare related tovier
    völl|ig, voll|ständigare related tovoll
    Vor|teil, vor|wärtsare related tovor
F - everything else (the default)

Note: Loanwords like Vase, Verb, Video, Visier, Vulkan, Kurve, Larve, brave use the English /v/ sound - excluded here.
