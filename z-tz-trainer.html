Z vs TZ - The /ts/ sound in German:

Both make the same /ts/ sound! The spelling depends on the vowel before:

Z - after long vowels, diphthongs (ei, au, eu), consonants, or at word start
Examples: tanzen (long a), Herz (after r), Zeit (word start)

TZ - after short vowels (never at word start!)
Examples: Katze (short a), sitzen (short i), Platz (short a)

Tip: Short vowels are usually followed by double consonants (tt, mm, ss) or tz/ck!
