German Anglicisms Quiz

Learn which English words Germans actually use

Real = Used in German
Fake = Not used / Different

German uses many English words (Anglicisms), but sometimes they mean something different, or Germans use completely different words than you'd expect!

Watch out for:
- False friends: "Handy" means mobile phone in German, not convenient!
- German-made English: "Homeoffice" (working from home) isn't used this way in English
- Different words: Germans say "Beamer" not "projector"
