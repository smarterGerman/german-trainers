Listen whether ST sounds like "sht" or "st".

The spelling is always ST, but pronunciation depends on position:
  • sht = ST at the beginning of a syllable: Stadt, Stein, verstehen
  • st = ST in the middle or end: fest, Gast, Fenster

The key is syllable structure. In "verstehen", the syllables are "ver-ste-hen" - ST starts a syllable, so it's "sht". In "Fenster", the syllables are "Fens-ter" - ST doesn't start a syllable, so it's "st".
