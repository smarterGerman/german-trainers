The German PF sound /pf/:

This is an affricate - both sounds (p + f) are pronounced together!

Word start: Pferd, Pflanze, Pfanne
Start with lips together (like "p"), then release into "f"

Word middle/end: Apfel, Kopf, Kampf
Same sound, often easier to pronounce

Common mistake: Saying just "f" instead of "pf" (Ferd instead of Pferd)
