PF or F?

Space Play F pf J f
Listen for the difference between PF and F.

German pf is a single sound (affricate) where you start with closed lips (like "p") and release into "f". English speakers often drop the "p" and just say "f".
  • pf: Pferd, Apfel, Kopf
  • f: Feld, Affe, Schaf
