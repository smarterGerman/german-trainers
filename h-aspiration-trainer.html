Learn when German "h" is pronounced or silent

Pronounced (Haus)
Silent (gehen)

In German, the letter "h" behaves differently depending on its position:

Pronounced /h/ - At the start of a word or syllable: Haus, Hund, Bahnhof
Silent (Dehnungs-h) - After a vowel, makes the vowel long: gehen, sehen, Zahn, mehr

Listen to each word and decide whether the "h" is pronounced (you hear an /h/ sound) or silent (just makes the previous vowel long).
