ss or ß?

Space Play F ss J ß
Listen whether the vowel before is short or long.

Both ss and ß sound the same. The spelling depends on the vowel before:
  • ss = after a short vowel: Wasser, müssen, Fluss
  • ß = after a long vowel: Straße, groß, süß
  • ß = after a diphthong: weiß, heiß, außen
Note: In Swiss German there is no ß - they write SS instead. In standard German you cannot replace ß with SS.
