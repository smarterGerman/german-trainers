The spelling is always SP, but pronunciation depends on position:
  • shp = SP at the beginning of a syllable: Spiel, Sprache, versprechen
  • sp = SP in the middle or end: Wespe, Knospe, krispig

The key is syllable structure. In "versprechen", the syllables are "ver-spre-chen" - SP starts a syllable, so it's "shp". In "Wespe", the syllables are "Wes-pe" - SP doesn't start a syllable, so it's "sp".
