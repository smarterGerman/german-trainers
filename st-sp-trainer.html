German ST/SP pronunciation rule:

At the beginning of a word or syllable, ST and SP are pronounced with a "sh" sound:
st = "scht" (like "sht")
sp = "schp" (like "shp")

Examples with "scht/schp":
Stadt = "Schtadt" (city)
Spiel = "Schpiel" (game)
verstehen = "ferschtehen" (understand)

In the middle or end (not at syllable start), keep the normal "st/sp":
fest = "fest" (not "fescht")
Wespe = "Wespe" (wasp)
